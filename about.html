What is TOAST?


TOAST is the Terms of Applicable Service Translator. Many people (us included!) cannot quickly read those long blocks of lawyer-ese text in a terms of service agreement when using a new software product. So, we tend to click "accept all terms" without really understanding what we are actually agreeing to.


With the TOAST, we hope to make terms of service simple. Using a combination of transliteration, icons, and other visual tools, we make reading terms of service easy and accesible for all. Please enjoy using the TOAST. If you have any comments/concerns, please send them our way!
